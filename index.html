About Me

Hi I am Patience, a data consultant with a passion for bringing clarity to messy, scattered, or paper-based information. I've supported community-led projects, NGOs, International NGOs, and small businesses to organize their data in ways that reduce stress and support better decision-making.

"I'm driven by empathy, structure, and the impact good systems can create."
